Frank's Fast Food Special

FRANK'S FAST FOOD EATERY
Hamburger with toppings $2.25
Fries .95
Cola .50
Coffee .75

Frank's Fast Food Eatery offers a Saturday Soccer Team Special. When student sports teams come in wearing their uniforms and accompanied by their coaches, they can get one hamburger free for every 5 they purchase.

The entire Meadowland team wore their uniforms and went to Frank's Eatery for lunch with their coaches. Each person had one hamburger with toppings. The players each had a cola and shared a bag of fries with one other teammate. The two coaches each had their own fries and coffee. The total bill (no tax) came to $42.10

How many players are on the team?
