Bookworm

Justin has a set of encyclopedias on his shelf. The volumes are arranged in order from left to right. The covers are 3/16 inch thick, and each volume is 1 1/2 inches thick. Unfortunately, a bookworm finds the encyclopedias. It starts at the first page of volume I and eats its way to the last page of volume II before Justin discovers it.

How far does the bookworm travel?
